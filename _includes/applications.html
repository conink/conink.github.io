Applications

InVisiSwitch works as a regular switch, by creating a magnetic field that responds to object that are within the vicinity of the switch, to turn on/off electrical appliances. It can be used on existing appliances, by removing the traditional switch and apply InVisiSwitch that are adhesive, onto a wall. The switch can be connected to a centralized component, by using transparent material, that are conductive. The Centralized component is used to uphold the magnetic field on the switch and toggle the existing electrical appliances accordingly.
